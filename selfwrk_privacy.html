This Privacy Policy explains how SelfWrk ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the SelfWrk application at https://mdmeyer21.github.io/SelfWrk. By using SelfWrk, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the SelfWrk service:

Data Type What We Collect Why We Collect It
Account Information Email address, account creation date To create and manage your account, send authentication links
Fitness Data Body weight, height, age, sex, activity level, workout logs, exercise sets and reps To power workout tracking, TDEE calculations, and progress charts
Nutrition Data Food entries, calorie and macro logs, saved foods To provide nutrition tracking and daily macro summaries
App Preferences Workout split selection, goals, theme settings, unit preferences To personalize your experience across devices
Payment Information Subscription status only — no card numbers or financial data To verify Pro subscription access. All payment data handled by Stripe.

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera, microphone, browsing history, or any data not listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide and operate the SelfWrk service
  • Sync your data across your devices when you are signed in
  • Calculate personalized fitness metrics such as TDEE, macro targets, and progress trends
  • Verify your subscription status and deliver Pro features
  • Respond to your support requests or account inquiries
  • Improve the App based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a PostgreSQL database platform that implements industry-standard security practices including encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL). Our database is hosted in the United States.

Access to your data is restricted to your account only, enforced by Row Level Security policies that prevent any user from accessing another user's data. We do not have staff who routinely access individual user data.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4. Payment Processing

SelfWrk uses Stripe, Inc. for all payment processing. When you subscribe to SelfWrk Pro:

  • Your payment card information is entered directly on Stripe's secure servers
  • SelfWrk never sees, receives, or stores your credit card number, CVV, or bank account details
  • We only receive confirmation of your subscription status (active, cancelled, or expired)
  • Stripe's Privacy Policy governs how your payment data is handled: https://stripe.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. Your data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Supabase: Our database provider stores your data as described above. Supabase processes data on our behalf under strict confidentiality terms.
  • Stripe: Your email address is shared with Stripe solely for the purpose of processing and managing your subscription.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

6. Cookies and Tracking

SelfWrk uses no third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics platforms. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar tracking technology.

The App uses browser localStorage to store your preferences and session data locally on your device. This is not a cookie and is not transmitted to any third party. Supabase uses a secure session token stored in localStorage to keep you logged in — this token is used only to authenticate requests to your own data.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will delete all associated personal data from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Anonymized, aggregate data that cannot identify any individual may be retained indefinitely for the purpose of improving the service.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format (available via the Export CSV feature in the App)
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at wrkforge@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
